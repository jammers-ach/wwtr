# Who Would They Rather?

Who would Alice rather *go on a date with*?

- A personification of the world's largest prime number  
- The donkey from *Shrek*  
- Six angry geese  
- A talking cat who is incredibly judgmental of your outfit choices  

## How does it work?

There are two types of cards: **Who** cards and **Rather** cards.

Each player is dealt **4 Who cards**. The remaining Who cards are placed face down in a draw pile.

The Rather cards are also placed face down in their own pile.

### Gameplay

One player becomes the judge for the round (for example, Alice).

Alice draws a **Rather** card and reads it out loud, for example:

> "Who would I rather go to for tax advice?"

Each other player looks at their Who cards and chooses the one they think Alice would pick.

They place their chosen Who card **face down** in the center of the table.

Once everyone has played a card, Alice shuffles the submitted cards **without looking at them**, then lays them out in front of her.

No one reveals which card they submitted.

Alice then chooses the card that best answers the question (the one she would *rather* pick).

The player who submitted that card reveals themselves and wins the round, keeping the Rather card as a point.

### Next round

The next player becomes the judge, draws a new Rather card, and everyone else draws a new Who card so they are back up to 4 cards.
